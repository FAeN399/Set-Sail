# Set-Sail Vision

Set-Sail is a playground for bold-yet-small experiments at the intersection of humans and AI copilots. We believe progress comes from charting many short voyages, shipping tangible artifacts, and writing down what we learn each time. The repo should feel like a living captain's log where anyone can suggest a heading and help steer the ship.

## Principles

1. **Bet Small, Learn Fast** — Scope voyages so they can launch within days, not weeks.
2. **Ship Artifacts** — Every voyage leaves behind code, prompts, or notes others can reuse.
3. **Document Learnings** — Capture hypotheses, surprises, and debriefs in the logbook.
4. **Invite Collaboration** — Keep the deck tidy with automation and clear docs so new sailors can join quickly.
5. **Stay Playful** — Maintain the spirit of exploration and curiosity in every commit.
